Why SecureAuth

Everything you need

Built with security best-practices, developer ergonomics, and a polished end-user experience in mind.

JWT Authentication

Secure httpOnly cookies with auto-refresh every 14 minutes. Tokens are verified server-side on every request.

bcrypt Hashing

Passwords are hashed with 12 salt rounds using bcryptjs. Plain-text passwords are never stored or transmitted.

Role-Based Access

User and Admin roles enforced both server-side and client-side. Admins get a full management dashboard.

Avatar Uploads

Profile pictures with circular crop tool, stored securely in MongoDB. Real-time preview before saving.

MongoDB Backend

Mongoose ODM with a full User model supporting username, email, phone, role, disabled status, and avatars.

Responsive Design

Tailwind CSS with a smooth slide-from-top sidebar menu, toast notifications, and AOS scroll animations.

Simple Process

How it works

1

Create your account

Sign up with a username, email, and password. Your password is immediately hashed with bcrypt before being stored.

2

Authenticate securely

Log in to receive a signed JWT stored in an httpOnly cookie — invisible to JavaScript, safe from XSS attacks.

3

Access your dashboard

View your account info, update your profile, upload a photo, and manage your password from a clean dashboard.
